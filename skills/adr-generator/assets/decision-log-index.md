## Statuses

* **Proposed** - Decision under consideration
* **Accepted** - Decision has been approved and is active
* **Deprecated** - Decision is no longer current but remains for historical context
* **Superseded** - Decision has been replaced by a newer ADR (link to new ADR)
* **Rejected** - Decision was considered but rejected (rare, usually just not proposed)
